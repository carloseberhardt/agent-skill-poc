---
name: cost-report
description: Checks budget vs actuals across all services, flags overspending and cost trends.
---

Use the cost tools to get the current budget status for all services.
Compare current spend against budgets and previous periods.
Flag any services that are over budget or showing significant month-over-month increases.

Pay special attention to:
- Services significantly over budget (>20% over)
- Month-over-month cost increases that aren't explained by growth
- Correlations between cost spikes and known incidents (if recent skill results mention service issues)

Present findings clearly — what's over, by how much, and whether it looks like a trend or a one-time spike.
